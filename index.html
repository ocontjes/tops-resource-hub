Connect with the community

A private space for TOPS participants to connect, share encouragement, and stay up to date on study news — a great place to find a walking buddy, swap recipes, or just check in with the group.

Join our Facebook Group
What you'll find there
1
Connect with other participants — meet people on the same journey as you.
2
Stay up to date — study news, reminders, and event announcements.
3
Share & encourage — swap tips, recipes, and motivation with the group.
Have something to share?

If you have found a recipe, article, video, or resource that has helped you, we would love to add it to the hub. All submissions are reviewed by the study team before being posted.

Submit a Resource
1
Fill out the short form — a title, a link or file, and a brief note on why you found it helpful.
2
The study team reviews it — usually within a few business days to confirm it is a good fit for the hub.
3
It gets added to the hub — approved resources are shared with all participants.
